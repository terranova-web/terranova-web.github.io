Guided by 'Right Plant Right Place', we aim to work with your existing garden conditions to create a scheme which suits your tastes.

Influenced by natural landscapes we advocate a high density style of planting. We aim to create a dynamic, dense planting scheme which will evolve and change with the seasons and years- as it would in nature.

This has several benefits: a mature planting quicker, less moisture evaporation from the soil, less soil erosion, less staking, less room for weeds to colonise and so less maintenance required.

Starting with the structure we develop a planting which performs through the seasons utilising the full range of plants- from bulbs through to grasses and biennials, advocating native species where appropriate.

We recognise that a planting is a living tapestry which over time will need editing or amplifying to create the right balance.

Careful use of self seeding plants, or biennials can be implemented to create exciting dynamics within a scheme, or a simple more pared down planting using a limited palette: both fit with the high density planting style.

At 'Terranova' we spend time growing and observing plants. This ongoing exploration allows us to be continually developing, and discovering more plants, their habits and how best to use them in our gardens.

Get in touch with us to make an appointment for a visit to your garden. We'll have an unformal chat about what you're planning to do and the potential of your garden in terms of planting.

If you want us to go ahead, we'll start working on a planting design that would match both with your taste and garden situation (sunny/shady site, type of soil, …). Leave us a couple of weeks to finalise the work (drawing, planting list, pictures) and send it to you either by post or email
